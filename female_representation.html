Female Role Models

Empowerment Through Representation

Women are much more likely to enter and remain in STEM if there are strong female role models present. Men are already represented as being highly successful in these fields, as evidenced by household names like Elon Musk or Bill Gates. When someone aspires to something, they naturally look for role models they can relate to. Since women must overcome different obstacles to succeed in STEM, it is difficult for them to relate to men like Musk and Gates.1
